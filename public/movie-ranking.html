The Best Movies according to Chandu Nallamothu

My top 3 Movies of All Time!

Black Panther

The CGI is superb and the plot is amazing. But I am very sad the actor passed away and I think that was the downfall for Marvel after that. That year in 8th grade I saw the best marvel movies ever produced other than Captain Marvel.

Cars

This was one that started my passion for cars so I love it. Even though it was sad that Doc passed away in the second one.

Mr. and Mrs. Smith

The acting between the couple is amazing with their on screen and off screen chemistry. I love secret agent movies in general so it was one that I enjoyed thouroughly. Although I have not yet seen the show on amazon prime yet.
